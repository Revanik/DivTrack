About Initial Investment
What is the Initial Investment?

This is the total amount of money you originally invested in dividend-paying securities (like MSTY, JEPQ, etc.). DivTrack uses this to calculate when you've "recovered" your principal through dividend income.

Example:
  • You invest $10,000 in dividend ETFs
  • Over time, you receive $10,000 in dividends
  • DivTrack flags this as "Principal Recovered"
  • Any additional dividends are "Post-Recovery Gains"
Why Track This?

Principal recovery is a key milestone in dividend investing. Once you've recovered your initial investment through dividends, you're essentially earning "free money" while still owning the underlying assets.
